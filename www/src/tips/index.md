# Tips

Pick a topic below to learn how that part of Dexter works.

## Plan your day

- [**Quick Planner**](/tips/quick-planner/). Filter, group, and search every
  task as you build your day.
- [**Day View**](/tips/day/). The single-day planner.
- [**Week View**](/tips/week/). Zoom out and plan the week ahead.
- [**Calendar**](/tips/calendar/). Pull events alongside your tasks.

## Capture

- [**Tasks**](/tips/tasks/). Status, due dates vs. do dates, lists, and more.
- [**Notes**](/tips/notes/). Lightweight notes attached to your planning.
- [**Journal**](/tips/journal/). Reflect on the day.

## Practice

- [**Priorities**](/tips/priorities/). The heart of the
  [Dexter method](/method).
- [**Habits**](/tips/habits/). Build routines that stick.

## Integrations

- [**MCP server**](/tips/mcp-server/). Connect Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and
  other AI assistants to your planning data.
